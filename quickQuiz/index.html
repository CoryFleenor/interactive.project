Rules of the quiz

  1. You will only have 15 seconds per question.
  2. Once you select your answer it can not be undone.
  3. You can't select any option once the timer goes off.
  4. You can't exit the quiz while you are playing.
  5. You'll get points off the correct answers.
  6. To replay the Quiz you have to use the replay quiz button at the top of the screen.
