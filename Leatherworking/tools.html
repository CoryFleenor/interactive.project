Knife

This goes without saying but you need a sharp knife to be able to cut the leather into whatever pattern you are working with. The rounded style pictured above is very popular with leather crafters today. Though i just use a simple box knife, as long as its sharp it will get the job done.

Something to punch the holes

You need something to put holes in the leather. Even if its just to add a buckle or clasp but with sewing you need something to make the holes. I prefer a single hole punch instead a diamond chiesel and awl, but to each there own. There is no wrong tool as long as it gets a hole in the leather!

Needles and Thread

Needles and thread. You need something to put your project together. The most common way is to saddle stich all your pieces in place. I prefer Ritza Tiger-thread over others but there are many different types to choose from and its all personal opinon on which is best.

Thats it thats the basic tools you need to do any project. There are many more like carving knifes, stamps, pliers, setters to bevelers and skiving knifes, but to do a simple project all you need is a knife, something to punch your holes, and something to hold the project together. A simple way to get started and fall down the rabbit hole called leather craft.
